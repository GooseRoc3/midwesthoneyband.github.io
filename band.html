Meet the Band

Six musicians. One loud night.

Midwest Honey is Chicago's pop rock and punk cover band — a 6-piece show ripping through hits from 1990 to today across bars and clubs in the Midwest.

Vocals

Sami

  • First concert: Sara Bareilles
  • Favorite song we play: "Iris" by The Goo Goo Dolls
  • When not with the band: Wedding crafts, life coaching, cooking, traveling, and hanging with my pals
  • Go-to jam song: "Silver Springs" by Fleetwood Mac
  • First album owned: Breakaway by Kelly Clarkson

Vocals

Tanya

  • First concert: Aerosmith (in Moscow)
  • Favorite song we play: "Bring Me to Life" by Evanescence
  • When not with the band: Vocal coaching, international competition judging, and mentoring for TV shows
  • Go-to jam song: "In and Out of Love" by Armin van Buuren
  • First album owned: American Idiot by Green Day

Guitar

Daniel

  • First concert: Cage the Elephant
  • Favorite song we play: "Kilby Girl" by The Backseat Lovers
  • When not with the band: Cooking, volunteering at the cat shelter, and fencing
  • Go-to jam song: "Man in the Box" by Alice in Chains
  • First album owned: Death Magnetic by Metallica

Guitar

Spencer

  • Favorite song we play: "Just a Girl" by No Doubt
  • When not with the band: Running, playing volleyball, and exploring new restaurants and breweries
  • Go-to jam song: "I Could Die for You" by Red Hot Chili Peppers
  • First album owned: Move Along by The All-American Rejects

Bass

Schafer

  • When not with the band: Cooking, trivia, lifting, biking, and pickleball

  • First album owned: The All-American Rejects by The All-American Rejects

Drums

Tommy

  • First album owned: 21st Century Breakdown by Green Day
